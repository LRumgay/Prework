Machine Girl

Bio

Machine Girl consists of two members, a producer/mixer and drummer. They are from New York City and went to highschool/college together. Both eventually dropped out of college to persue their passion. Machine girl is know for it's simplicity but also hard hitting bass lines, as well as being able to mix well with simple melodic samples.
